Planning Domain Definition Language (PDDL)

11:00 - 12:30 – Francisco J. Rodríguez Lera

An introduction to PDDL, the standard language for defining domains and problems in automated planning. The session will cover the core concepts, syntax, and structure of the language, along with basic examples to illustrate how actions, states, and goals are modelled in a plannable environment.
